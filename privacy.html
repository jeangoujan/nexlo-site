Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 12, 2025

Thank you for using Evolv (“the App”, “we”, “our”, or “us”). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Evolv does not collect, share, or sell any personal data. All data — such as your sessions, notes, and skills — is stored locally on your device only. We do not use external analytics or advertising SDKs.

2. Local Data Storage

Evolv uses secure local storage on your device to keep your:

  • Practice sessions and durations
  • Notes and skill statistics
  • App preferences (e.g., theme and timer duration)

This data never leaves your device unless you manually back it up.

3. Notifications

Evolv may send local notifications (for example, when your timer ends). These are handled entirely on your device and never pass through external servers.

4. Third-Party Services

Evolv does not connect to any external APIs or third-party platforms.

5. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is nothing to access, delete, or share. Uninstalling the app will permanently remove all stored information.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
support@evolvapp.site

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
